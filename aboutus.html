Thrissur Show Room

Radhia Motors name made its debut in Thrissur's Commercial life since 1978 with Bajaj Tempo Authorised Dealership named M/s. Sastha Auto Sales & Service at Punkunnam, Thrissur. (Guruvayoor Road).

A Company, its culture and its character are all a reflection of its founder. Radhia Motors took shape in the mind and heart of our founder Shri. E.Gopinatha Kurup. He conceived the organization, fueled it with his vision and steered its growth and today Radhia Motors is a trusted and revered automobile ogranisation with huge customers base.

Palakkad Show Room

Over a period of time the organization spread its wings and extended operations also in the neighboring Palakkad district way in 1993. Today, with their enormous resources, Radhia Group is one of the largest and most professionally managed Authorised Commercial Automobile Dealership in Thrissur and Palakkad districts dealing Goods and Passenger vehicles of M/s. Force Motors Ltd. with the business footprint in the central part of Kerala.
